Who Am I?

I'm a final-year Computer Science undergraduate at AIUB (American International University-Bangladesh), specializing in LLM systems, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, Computer Vision, and applied deep learning. I am currently working as an AI Automation Intern at Infinoz while actively exploring internship, graduate, and full-time AI engineering opportunities.

My work combines research depth with delivery: from YOLO-based vision systems and FastAPI services to multi-agent applications, n8n automations, and Supabase-backed RAG pipelines designed for real users and practical deployment.

Alongside hands-on product work, I have authored 9 papers across CORE-ranked and international venues, including two newly accepted first-author papers now under consideration for inclusion in a Taylor & Francis book, and I serve as an IEEE conference reviewer in deep learning, intelligent systems, and applied AI.

  • Skin & lab analysis (Gemini Vision API)
  • Health timeline, disease alerts, admin analytics
  • Streaming LLM chat, RAG with Bangladesh/WHO sources
