Who Am I?

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate at AIUB (American International University–Bangladesh), specializing in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Large Language Models.

As a published IEEE author with papers at CORE-ranked international venues, I bridge cutting-edge research with hands-on engineering — from YOLO-based detection pipelines to LLM agents built with LangChain and LangGraph.

Beyond research, I serve as an active IEEE conference reviewer, evaluating manuscripts in deep learning, intelligent systems, and applied AI for conferences like 3ICT 2025 and FET'26.
